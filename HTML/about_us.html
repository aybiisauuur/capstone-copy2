About Filipino Sign Language (FSL)

FSL is used by Filipino deaf or hard-of-hearing individuals. It is a visual language that combines gestures, facial expressions along with hand and body movements.

It is neither based on nor resembles Filipino and English, since Sign Language is a visual language that relies heavily on imagery and concepts.

About the Application

The modules included in this app is useful for conversational and basic FSL, and includes specific phrases for the use of COMELEC-CAR workers.
